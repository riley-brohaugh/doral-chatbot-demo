Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay my water bill?

Water bills can be paid online through our payment portal, by phone, mail, or in person at City Hall during business hours.

When is trash collection in my area?

Trash collection schedules vary by neighborhood. Please visit our Public Works page or call 311 for your specific collection day.

How do I obtain a building permit?

Building permits can be applied for online or in person at the Building Department. Required documents include construction plans and proof of ownership.

What are the city's park hours?

Most city parks are open from sunrise to sunset. Some facilities have extended hours for scheduled activities and events.
